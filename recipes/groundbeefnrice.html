Ground Beef with Rice

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Melted butter
  • Olive oil
  • Chopped onions
  • Minced garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rice
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil and melted butter in a medium-sized.
  2. Add onions to the pan. Cook on medium-high heat for 13-15 minutes, allowing the onions to caramelize. Stir occasionally.
  3. When halfway through cooking onions, add garlic to the pan. Stir and allow onions and garlic to continue to cook until soft and lightly brown. Reduce heat to low.
  4. Add a pinch of both salt and pepper, or to taste.
  5. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a separate medium-sized pan. Add beef, stirring frequently.
  6. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until beef is browned and has absorbed most of the moisture in the pan. Remove from heat
  7. Add the olives to the pan of cooked onions. Stir to combine and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add cooked beef to olive and onion mixture. Stir to combine and cook for additional 1-2 minutes.
  9. Serve with white rice.
  10. Enjoy!
