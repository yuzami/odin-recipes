Spaghetti

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • ground beef
  • olive oil
  • tomato paste
  • basil

Steps

  1. Cook your ground beef until brown
  2. Add the tomato sauce on top of it
  3. Cook the noodles al dente
  4. add them to the sauce
  5. serve immediately with the basil
