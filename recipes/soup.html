Steps

  1. Press the SAUTE button on the Instant Pot
  2. Add 2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil to the pot.
  3. Once the oil is hot, arrange 1 pound (500 g) of rinsed boneless skinless chicken breast in the pot in a single layer.
  4. Brown the chicken by searing for 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside on a plate.
  6. Add ½ cup chopped onions and 2 teaspoon minced garlic to the pot, and sauté for 3-4 minutes, until the onions start to brown.
  7. Add the following ingredients and cook for 2-3 minutes.
    • 1 cup of cubed carrots (¼-inch cubes)
    • 1 cup of cubed potatoes (½-inch cubes)
    • 1 piece of corn (cut into 4 pieces)
  8. Now add the following ingredients and stir until combined.
    • 5 cups of chicken broth
    • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
    • ½ teaspoon cumin powder
    • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
    • ½ teaspoon chili powder
    • 1 whole bay leaf
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon black pepper powder
  9. Place the chicken breasts back into the pot and close the lid.
  10. Set the valve to the sealing position.
  11. Press CANCEL and then press PRESSURE COOK.
  12. Set the timer to 8 minutes at high pressure. The Instant Pot will take 10-12 minutes to build the pressure and start the timer.
  13. Once the timer goes off, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes.
  14. Release the remaining pressure manually and open the lid.
  15. Discard the bay leaf, then transfer the chicken breasts to a chopping board using a pair of tongs.
  16. Shred the chicken using a fork.
  17. Add the shredded chicken back to the pot.
  18. Press the SAUTE button once again.
  19. Add 2 oz (60 g) of Angel hair pasta and 1 tablespoon of lime juice to the pot.
  20. Cook for 6-7 minutes or until the noodles are just cooked (al dente).
  21. Transfer the soup to serving bowls. Garnish with chopped scallion (green onions) or cilantro and serve hot.
